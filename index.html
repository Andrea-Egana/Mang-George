Welcome to Mang George!


The game challenges players to survive intense bullet hell boss battles while growing stronger through strategic upgrades after each fight. Players must face powerful bosses with unique attack patterns, dodge and avoid incoming attacks using movement, jumping, and an invincibility dash, and adapt to the bosses evolving phases as the battles become more difficult
